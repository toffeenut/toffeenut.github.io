PackPoint · Drinkpoint
Given extreme flexibility, I was asked to come up with a design for an app that 'packs your bag based on the weather'. With the rare chance to be in complete control, I started from bullet points, proceeded to sketches and wireframes, designed the mocks-ups, and finally prepared the production-ready assets.

Days after its iOS release, it was featured as one of Apple's Best New Apps.

Also available on Google Play, PackPoint was prepared for Android and its many screen resolutions. Soon, it will also be released for the iPad and various Android tablets.
