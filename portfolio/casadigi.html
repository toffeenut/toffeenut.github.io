Casadigi Home Automation · Paragon Business Solutions
Coming from the success of Digivalet Hotel Automation, the same client asked me to come up with a home solution given roughly the same feature requirements. Unlike the hotel app where users were short-staying guests who were typically first-timers, the home app had the luxury of more space, nested screens, gesture control and personalization.

The result became a very modern, minimalist, intuitive interface, available in both mobile and tablet resolutions.
