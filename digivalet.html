Digivalet

Broke through the hospitality industry

Digivalet is a full hotel automation solution for luxury hotels. In 2008, we were brought in to guide the user experience design of multiple iOS-based apps and web-based backends and dashboards.


Simplifying the complex

Behind the Scenes

Digivalet has come a long way from the 1st version nearly 10 years ago. With its now dozens of features, the challenge was to simplify the complex structure enough, to be a pick-up-and-use type of product especially for a short-term hotel guest.
